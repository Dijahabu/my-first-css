Best spots to cry on campus!

There is no greater feeling than getting on a Campus Connector in the winter, just after sunset.
The bus is mostly empty, the lights are on and the bus is just warm enough that you can slip off your pair of gloves to shuffle a Spotify playlist and stare dreamily out the window. It is in these moments of peace that members of the student body can feel safe... safe enough to cry. Many generations of college students have taken a break in the connector to feel the feels, as the saying goes. At this point, crying in the back of a campus connector is a rite of passage for any hopeful graduate.

  • The top floor of Northrup
  • The first floor of the Student Union (when you are a pianist)
  • Second floor of the Weisman on the balcony
  • The basement bathrooms of the Rec
